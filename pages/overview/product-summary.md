# Product Summary

AccessiWay Platform is a centralized tool for managing digital accessibility across all websites and digital applications. It's built for project manager, developers  and compliance managers who need to identify, prioritize, and fix accessibility issues without juggling spreadsheets, audit PDFs, and disconnected workflows.

The platform organizes everything around **touchpoints** (websites and apps). For each touchpoint, you get a clear picture of accessibility issues, remediation progress, and compliance status against WCAG 2.2 (levels A and AA) and EN 301 549.

Instead of treating accessibility as a one-time audit, AccessiWay gives to the clients a living dashboard that updates in real time. Client team fixes issues, requests expert validation, and tracks compliance improvements all from one workspace.
