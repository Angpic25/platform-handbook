# Product Summary

AccessiWay Platform is a SaaS B2B solution for digital accessibility management. It allows organizations (our clients) to monitor, manage, and improve the accessibility of their digital touchpoints (websites, web apps, and digital services).

The platform is designed for product managers, developers, and compliance managers who need to track accessibility issues, coordinate fixes with their teams, and demonstrate compliance with international standards such as WCAG 2.2 (levels A and AA) and EN 301 549.

Each client organization (Account) can manage multiple touchpoints. For each touchpoint, the platform provides a detailed dashboard with compliance metrics, issue tracking, trend analysis, and integration with project management tools like Jira.

AccessiWay experts periodically audit each touchpoint and populate the platform with identified issues, suggested fixes, and compliance data. The client team can then use the platform to prioritize, assign, and track the resolution of each issue.
