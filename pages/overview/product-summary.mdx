# Product Summary

AccessiWay Platform is the tool we provide to our clients to manage digital accessibility in a continuous and structured way. It is not simply a report: it is a workspace where the client can see the real state of their accessibility, monitor progress over time, and coordinate fixes with their internal team.

## The problem it solves (NOW)

Many organizations receive accessibility audits as static documents that are difficult to update and share internally. Issues end up in spreadsheets, fixes go untracked, and demonstrating progress to management or regulators becomes a manual effort. The platform solves this by centralizing everything in a single, always up-to-date interface.

## How it supports the client

When AccessiWay completes an audit, issues are loaded directly into the platform, already classified by severity, WCAG criterion, and user impact. From that point, the client has everything they need to act: they know what to fix first, can read the fix suggested by our experts, assign corrections to their team via Jira, and request a retest to have the work validated.

The platform supports the client at every stage, from identifying the problem to having the fix certified by an AccessiWay expert. A cycle that previously required weeks of email exchanges now happens in one place.

## Who uses it on the client side

Within the client organization, the platform is designed for three main profiles: project managers or digital leads who need a clear view of compliance status, developers who need precise instructions on what to fix and how, and compliance managers who need to demonstrate progress.
