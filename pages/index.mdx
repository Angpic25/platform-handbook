# AccessiWay Platform Handbook

Welcome to the AccessiWay Platform Handbook, the internal reference guide for everyone at AccessiWay who works with the platform.

This handbook is designed for anyone in the team who needs to understand how the platform works, stay up to date on new releases, or find answers to the most common product questions.

The product team maintains this guide and keeps it updated. If you have suggestions on the content, they are always welcome.

This handbook is for internal use only. Do not share its contents outside of AccessiWay.
