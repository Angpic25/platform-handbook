# Project Managers

This section is the go-to reference for Project Managers who work with the AccessiWay Platform day to day. It covers the key workflows, processes, and answers to the most common questions that come up when managing client onboarding and delivery.

While it is designed with PMs in mind, anyone at AccessiWay who wants to understand how the platform fits into our client workflows will find it useful.

## What you will find here

- **Onboarding Flow** — the end-to-end process for getting a client up and running on the platform after a new audit is ready
- **Delivery Call** — how to present the platform to the client during the delivery call
- **FAQ** — answers to the most common questions about platform mechanics, retests, and account management
