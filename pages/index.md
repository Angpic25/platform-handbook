# Welcome to AccessiWay Platform Docs

AccessiWay Platform is your centralized workspace for managing digital accessibility. It helps you identify high-impact issues, coordinate fixes with your team and accessibility experts, and track compliance with WCAG 2.2 — all in one place.

This documentation covers everything you need to get up and running.
