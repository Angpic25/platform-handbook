# Issue Management

Find the issues that matter most — search, filter, sort, and export your full issue list.

<!-- 🎥 VIDEO NEEDED: Using search, applying filters, sorting by severity, and exporting to CSV, ~30 seconds -->
*[Video: Searching and filtering issues]*

## How it works

- **Search** by issue ID or description (min. 3 characters)
- **Filter** by severity, status, type, WCAG criteria, pages, comments, and mitigation
- **Quick filters** for common views: Open issues, Critical issues, Recurring issues
- **Sort** any column (ID, WCAG criteria, Severity, Status, Comments, Mitigation, Type)
- **Export** the full or filtered issue list as CSV/Excel

## Step by step

### 1. Search or apply filters

Use the search bar or click **Filters** to open the advanced panel.
