Your starting point — see company-wide accessibility status, key metrics, and which touchpoints need attention first.

## How it works

- **Active critical issues** shows how many critical issues remain open across all touchpoints
- **Resolved issues** tracks how many issues your team has fully resolved
- **Highest-impact touchpoints** surfaces the top 3 touchpoints by accessibility impact
- Click **View all touchpoints** to jump to the full list
- Each touchpoint row shows domain, compliance %, and open issue count

> **💡 Tip**
> Start your day here — the highest-impact table tells you exactly where to focus.
