# Touchpoints List

Browse every touchpoint in your account — see compliance, audit dates, and total issues at a glance.

![Touchpoints list](/images/placeholder-touchpoints-list.png)

## How it works

- Each row shows **domain**, **audit date**, **compliance %**, and **total issues**
- Click any row to open that touchpoint's detail view
- Adjust rows per page (default: 25) with the pagination control
- Sorted by domain by default

> **💡 Tip**
> Look for touchpoints with low compliance % and high issue counts — those are your quick wins.
