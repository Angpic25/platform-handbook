# Settings & Team

Set up 2FA for your account and manage who has access to the workspace.

![Settings modal](/images/placeholder-settings-modal.png)
<!-- 📸 CAPTURE NEEDED: Settings modal showing Security and Team Members tabs -->

## How it works

- **Security** — enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) via QR code for extra account protection
- **Team Members** — view, invite, and remove workspace members
- Members have a **role** (e.g. User) and a **status** (Active or Pending)
- Settings open as a modal overlay — you don't leave your current page

## Step by step

### 1. Open Settings

Click **Settings** in the left sidebar. The modal appears on top of your current view.

![Settings link](/images/placeholder-settings-link.png)
<!-- 📸 CAPTURE NEEDED: Left sidebar with Settings link highlighted -->

### 2. Set up 2FA

On the **Security** tab, click **Setup 2FA** and scan the QR code with your authenticator app.

<!-- 🎥 VIDEO NEEDED: 2FA setup flow — clicking Setup 2FA, scanning QR code, entering verification code, ~20 seconds -->
*[Video: Setting up Two-Factor Authentication]*

### 3. Manage team members

Switch to the **Team Members** tab. Click **Add team member** to invite someone by email, or use the trash icon to remove a member.

![Team members list](/images/placeholder-team-members.png)

> **💡 Tip**
> Invited members show as "Pending" until they accept — no action needed from you.
