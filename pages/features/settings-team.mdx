# Settings and Team

The Settings panel is accessible from the left sidebar and opens as a modal overlay. It contains two sections: Security and Team Members.

![Settings panel showing security and team management options](/images/placeholder-settings.png)

## What the client sees

- **Two Factor Authentication (2FA)** setup with QR code configuration for added account security.
- **Team members table** showing name, email, role, and status (Active or Pending) for each member.
- **Add team member** button to invite new members via email.
- **Remove member** option (trash icon) to revoke access for a team member.
- **Single Owner** per account: the Account owner is the primary administrator who can invite and manage other team members.
- **Unlimited members**: there is no limit to the number of team members that can be invited to the account.
- **Roles and permissions**: team members are assigned roles that determine their level of access within the platform.

<Steps>

### Open Settings

The client clicks "Settings" in the left sidebar to open the settings modal.


### Configure 2FA

In the Security tab, the client clicks "Setup 2FA" and scans the QR code with an authenticator app to enable two factor authentication.

<video src="/images/video-2fa-setup.mp4" autoPlay loop muted playsInline style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '8px' }} />

### Manage team members

In the Team Members tab, the client can view all members, invite new ones, or remove existing members.

![Team members table showing name, email, role, and status](/images/placeholder-team-members.png)

</Steps>

<Callout>
The Account owner is the only user who can invite or remove team members. There is no limit to the number of members that can be added.
</Callout>
