# Issue Detail

Click any issue to see exactly what's wrong, how to fix it, and whether it's linked to Jira.

<!-- 🎥 VIDEO NEEDED: Opening an issue detail panel and navigating through Overview, Fix, and Jira tabs, ~20 seconds -->
*[Video: Exploring an issue detail]*

## How it works

- **Overview tab** — screenshot, severity, WCAG criteria, page location, mitigation status, and action buttons (Resolve / Dismiss)
- **Fix tab** — suggested code-level fix and expert comments
- **Jira tab** — linked Jira ticket and its current status

## Step by step

### 1. Open the detail panel

Click any row in the issues table. The side panel slides in from the right.

![Issue detail — Overview](/images/placeholder-issue-overview-tab.png)

### 2. Check the suggested fix

### 3. Check Jira status
