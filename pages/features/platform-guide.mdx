# Platform Guide

The Platform Guide is an in app onboarding section that helps the client understand how to use the platform effectively. It is accessible from the left sidebar navigation.

![Platform guide page showing onboarding content and FAQ section](/images/placeholder-platform-guide.png)

## What the client sees

- **How insights turn into action**: three core capabilities explained (centralize accessibility work, prove compliance with confidence, verify fixes with expert review).
- **What the platform enables**: a summary of key actions the client can take, from identifying issues to managing them in Jira.
- **Built in FAQ**: answers to common questions about compliance, retesting, WCAG coverage, and more.
- **Navigation guidance**: helps new users understand how to move between different sections of the platform.

## Frequently asked questions

| Question | Summary |
|----------|---------|
| What is this platform showing me? | A dynamic dashboard with compliance score, total issues, severity breakdown, and remediation progress. Data updates in real time. |
| Why prioritize by user impact instead of volume? | Prioritizing by user impact helps focus on real barriers rather than technically valid but low impact issues. |
| What is the difference between "fixed" and "resolved"? | Fixed means addressed by the dev team. Resolved means expert validated via a retest. |
| How does the accessibility retest work? | Up to 1 retest per touchpoint. The expert reviews the entire touchpoint to verify all fixes meet accessibility requirements. |
| How is the compliance percentage calculated? | Based on identified issues and their severity. It is an improvement indicator, not a legal certification. |
