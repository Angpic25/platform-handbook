# Onboarding Flow

There are two distinct flows depending on whether the client is accessing the platform for the first time or receiving a new audit on an existing account.

<Tabs items={['New customer or first time in platform', 'Second touchpoints onwards']}>

<Tabs.Tab>

## New customer or existing customer (first time in platform)

This flow starts when a deal is closed or a renewal is purchased, and ends with the client's first login.

<Steps>

### Decide: new audit or update existing?

The PM and the expert align on whether to start from scratch or build on a previous analysis.

- **Website changed significantly or is completely new** — the expert creates a new audit from scratch
- **Similar website, efficient to build on** — the expert starts from an existing analysis

### Upload to Chrome Extension

The expert uploads the audit data via the Chrome Extension.

### Send to platform — audit ready

The expert marks the audit as ready and sends it to the platform using the "Send to Platform" button in the Chrome Extension.

![Chrome Extension showing the Send to Platform button highlighted for a Wesfarmers audit](/images/onboarding-send-to-platform.png)

### Notification via it7-platform-notification-bot

When the expert clicks "Send to Platform", the product team receives a Slack notification in the `#it7-platform-notification-bot` channel.

![Slack notification from Accessiway Platform Bot showing a new analysis created in production with organisation, touchpoint, and tier details](/images/onboarding-slack-notification.png)

At this point the product team:

- **Creates the client organization and account** if this is the first time the company enters the platform. The platform owner's email must already be available at this stage.
- **Creates a support account** at `accessibility+client@accessiway.com` so that the PM and Expert can access and view the platform content for each individual client.
- Once both steps are completed, the Accessibility team is notified inside `#it7-platform-notification-bot` so they can proceed with organizing the delivery call for clients on Silver or Gold plans.
- For all subsequent touchpoints of the same company, every time the expert clicks "Send to Platform" a new notification is sent to `#it7-platform-notification-bot` and the product team adds the new touchpoint to the correct organization.

### Present results and create client account

The PM presents the platform results to the client during the scheduled call and creates the client account independently during the session.

### First login — onboarding video shown on entry

The client completes their first login. An onboarding video is shown automatically on entry.

</Steps>

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab>

## Second touchpoints onwards

This flow applies when a new audit is ready for a client who is already on the platform. No PM intervention is needed.

<Steps>

### New audit available

The expert has completed a new audit and it is ready to be published.

### Send to platform — audit ready

The expert marks the audit as ready and sends it to the platform.

### Notification via it7-dev-platform-bot

The product team receives a Slack notification via `#it7-dev-platform-bot` confirming the audit is live.

### Transactional email automatically sent to client

A transactional email is triggered automatically and sent to the client.

### Client logs in independently

The client receives the email and accesses the platform on their own. No PM intervention is needed.

</Steps>

</Tabs.Tab>

</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
As a first point of contact with the client, the PM sends an onboarding email containing a form that asks the client to indicate who the platform Owner will be. This information is needed before the product team can create the client account.
</Callout>
